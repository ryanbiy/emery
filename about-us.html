About us

Who We Are

Ukulima Agrovet Services Limited is the leading agrochemicals, veterinary and seeds solution company committed to the provision of quality, genuine and affordable farm inputs and services. The company is a leading and growing agribusiness service provider offering a wide range of services in the agricultural sector. Since 2002, we have endeavored to become Centre of excellence in promoting sustainable production in the agricultural sector. We continue to invest in extensive research to ensure services offered meets the need of our target customers while delivering high quality services through use of our highly qualified and motivated staff. We are well networked both locally and globally through research institutions, industry actors, farmer and farmer’s organization. We aim at being the leading farming specialists in Eastern Africa; offering unrivalled farming techniques which are cost effective through use of latest research and technology thereby ensuring sustainable returns to our clients.

Some of the factors that lead to our company growth are:
   Ensuring best and quality products to our customers i.e. providing improved hybrid seeds.
   We offer best and equal customer services to both small and large scale farmers without discrimination.
   We usually work to build a long term relationship with our customers and partners that are founded upon:
         • Transparency
         • Accountability of our actions
         • Learning from each other.
         • Openness and Flexibility
         • Delivering on our Promises
         • Honesty
         • High Performance Standards.
         • Reliability
Ukulima Agrovet family offers services to several counties in Kenya through Strategic partnerships with some of local and international seed companies. We are also actively involved in researches and introduction of products into the market that are geared towards increasing food sufficiency and safety for the well been of kenyan communities.
